# Book Specification: Cultures of Pakistan

Title: Cultures of Pakistan
Author: [Rabia Khan]
Target Audience: Students, travelers, 
Tone: Engaging, informative, respectful 

Structure:
1. Introduction
2. Chapter 1: Punjab (Focus: Lahore)
3. Chapter 2: Sindh (Focus: Karachi)
4. Chapter 3: Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (Focus: Peshawar)
5. Chapter 4: Balochistan (Focus: Quetta)
6. Chapter 5: Gilgit-Baltistan & Azad Kashmir
7. Conclusion
8. Glossary + Suggested Places

Rules for Every Culture:
- Description (customs, dresses, dances, festivals)
- History & Origin 
- Cultural importance 
- Engaging language
