Sindh's culture draws from the Indus Valley Civilization, with strong Sufi influences and symbols like Ajrak and Topi. Karachi blends urban energy with traditional shrines.
Iconic elements: Ajrak (block-printed shawl) and Sindhi Topi (embroidered cap), Jhumar dance, and festivals like Sindh Cultural Day (Ekta Day, wearing Ajrak/Topi) and Urs of Shah Abdul Latif Bhittai (music and dhamaal).
History and Origin: Ancient Mohenjo-Daro roots, Arab arrival bringing Islam, and Sufi saints shaping devotional traditions.
Cultural Importance: Promotes peace, tolerance, and unity; Sufi shrines are centers of spiritual gatherings, with qawwali music.
